Welcome

This interactive hunt invites you to explore seven mysteries inspired by the works of artist Toru Kuranuki. Hidden across this digital landscape are seven Echo Stations, each presenting a riddle based on legendary stones. Solve the riddle to collect a digital Echo Token. Once you collect all seven, the secrets of the Symphony of Stones will be revealed.

To begin your journey, click the button below to visit the first Echo Station. As you progress, hints will guide you to the next station. Your progress is stored locally in your browser, so you can return to continue any time.
